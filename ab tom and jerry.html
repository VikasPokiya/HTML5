Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is an American animated franchise and series of comedy short films.

Nationality

American

Born

created in 1940

Creator

William Hanna and Joseph Barbera.

Awards

In its original run, Hanna and Barbera produced 114 Tom and Jerry shorts for MGM from 1940 to 1958. During this time, they won seven Academy Awards for Animated Short Film, tying for first place with Walt Disney's Silly Symphonies with the most awards in the category.
